How It Works

Precision at the point of signing.

HelpU integrates via standard CDS Hooks — no custom builds, no workflow disruption. It works where clinicians already work.

01 —

Note is written

The clinician completes their note as normal — no extra steps, no forms, no interruptions during documentation.

02 —

Sign is clicked

At the exact moment the clinician clicks Sign, HelpU fires — analyzing the diagnosis against the documented clinical picture.

03 —

Review & correct

If a discrepancy is found, HelpU surfaces a plain-language card with clinical reasoning. The clinician decides — HelpU never blocks.

Why HelpU

Built for the moment that matters.

Most errors happen not from lack of knowledge — but from cognitive load at the end of a long note. HelpU closes that gap.
